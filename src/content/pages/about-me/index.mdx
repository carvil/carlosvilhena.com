Hi there! My name is Carlos, and I am a [Product and Engineering Leader](https://www.linkedin.com/in/carlosvilhena/) based in Portugal. I’m into startups, investment, and CrossFit.

With a background in Software Engineering, I have commercial Product, Engineering and Management experience for high-growth startups, established businesses, and public institutions across Europe for the past 18 years.

I have previously [co-founded a SaaS company](https://techcrunch.com/2016/03/08/the-final-delivery/), helped build better [government services](https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/government-digital-service), [scaled distributed engineering teams](https://www.yld.io), helped [Talkdesk](https://www.talkdesk.com) end bad customer service worldwide, and built [Astrada](https://astrada.co/)’s first API platform to enable access to real-time card transaction feeds on any credit card.

I love solving complex problems, growing product and engineering teams, taking projects from concept to release, and working with fast-paced teams to deliver value and scalable solutions.
