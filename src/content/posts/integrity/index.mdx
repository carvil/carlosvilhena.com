The more I think about it, the more I believe integrity is one of the most important core values to have.

Being known for candor, authenticity, and transparency.

> True integrity is a kind of secret leadership weapon
>
> Robert Iger in <Aff slug="the-ride-of-a-lifetime">The Ride of a Lifetime</Aff>

It simplifies everything regarding decision making. It makes things simple. And simple things are generally better.

Yet, so many people complicate what’s simple. Sometimes intentionally, sometimes not.

But regardless of the intentions, those on the receiving end will always read in between the lines.

And that’s the moment trust is lost. And once trust is lost, you have a mountain to climb.
